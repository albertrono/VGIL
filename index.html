FAQS

What services does Velie Group International offer?

We offer a range of services, including real estate solutions, recruitment and consultation, specialized training programs, and mental health advocacy. Our goal is to empower individuals and businesses by providing reliable, professional, and innovative solutions.

What industries do you recruit for?

We recruit skilled and unskilled professionals across multiple industries, including healthcare, construction, hospitality, IT, engineering, and domestic work. Our recruitment services extend to countries such as Dubai, Qatar, Australia, Canada, the United Kingdom, and many others.

Do you assist with work permits and visa processing?

Yes, we provide comprehensive assistance with document verification, work permit applications, visa processing, and legal compliance. We also offer pre-departure orientation and facilitate employment contracts for a smooth international job transition.

What types of training programs do you offer?

We offer specialized training programs, including healthcare training, first aid and manual handling, professional certifications, vocational training, and soft skills development. Our training is designed to enhance workforce competency and career growth.

How does Velie Group promote mental health advocacy?

We are dedicated to mental health awareness through corporate workshops, counseling services, stress management programs, and community outreach. Our initiatives aim to foster resilience, well-being, and mental health support in workplaces and communities.

Why should I choose Velie Group International?

Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and personalized service sets us apart. With a proven track record of successful placements, global partnerships, and industry expertise, we are your trusted partner for real estate, recruitment, training, and mental health advocacy.

How can I get in touch with Velie Group International?

You can reach us via email at ceo@veliehealthcare.com.au or call us at +254 720 044 388. We are based in Kenya and are always ready to assist you with your inquiries and service needs.
